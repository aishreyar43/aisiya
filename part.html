About AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include problem-solving, decision making, language processing, and pattern recognition.

AI has a wide range of applications, including robotics, machine learning, natural language processing (NLP), and deep learning, among others. As AI continues to evolve, it holds the potential to revolutionize industries such as healthcare, finance, education, and many more.
